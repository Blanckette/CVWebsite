Shopify online art gallery

objective

I set up a Shopify store to display and sell my oil paintings.

Through this process, I gained valuable experience in creating and optimizing website assets for better speed and performance. I delved into SEO techniques and understood the significance of a mobile-first approach in search optimization.

Additionally, I became proficient with the Shopify environment and experimented with personalizing templates to better suit my needs.

technologies used in project

For who Art galleries and artists.
Purpose To showcase artists portfolio and sell artworks.

inspiration

To design a polished and professional artist website , I drew inspiration from renowned art galleries like Gagosian.

These leading galleries often adopt a minimalist design approach, which effectively highlights the artwork without distraction.

By focusing on clean lines and a refined aesthetic , I aimed to create a website that emphasizes the art itself, ensuring that the presentation of my oil paintings would captivate visitors and align with the high standards set by established art institutions.
